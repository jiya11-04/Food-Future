Our Mission

We connect surplus food from providers like restaurants, stores, and individuals with people in need, helping reduce waste and provide meals to those who need them the most.

How It Works

Step 1

Food providers donate surplus food to our platform.

Step 2

Volunteers collect and organize the donations.

Step 3

The food is distributed to people in need through our network.
