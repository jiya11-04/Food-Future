Common Questions

What is Food Future?

Food Future is an initiative aimed at reducing food waste and ensuring food reaches those in need.

How can I donate food?

You can donate food by signing up as a donor through our website and scheduling a donation pickup or drop-off.

How do I volunteer?

You can volunteer by registering on our website. We have various opportunities, from food collection to distribution.

Can I donate money?

Yes! Your financial contributions help us cover logistics and operational costs. Visit our donation page for more details.

How is the food stored and distributed?

Food donations are stored in safe, temperature-controlled environments before being distributed to local shelters and communities in need.

If you have more questions or need assistance, feel free to contact us.
